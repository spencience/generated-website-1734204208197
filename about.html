About HippoPics

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a group of passionate wildlife photographers and hippo enthusiasts, HippoPics has grown into the web's premier destination for hippopotamus content. Our mission is to share the beauty, power, and charm of these magnificent creatures with the world.

What started as a small collection of personal photographs has blossomed into a vibrant community of hippo lovers, contributing amazing shots from around the globe.
