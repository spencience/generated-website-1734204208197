Fascinating Hippo Facts

Swimming Experts

Despite weighing up to 3,000 kg, hippos can swim at speeds of up to 8 km/h and can hold their breath underwater for up to 5 minutes!

Unique Diet

Hippos can consume up to 40 kg of grass in a single night! They're herbivores who prefer to graze during the cooler evening hours.

Natural Sunscreen

Hippos secrete a red-colored substance that acts as a natural sunscreen and antiseptic, protecting their sensitive skin.

Social Structure

Hippos live in groups called pods, usually consisting of 10-20 individuals led by a dominant male.

Impressive Teeth

Their canine teeth can grow up to 50 cm long and continue growing throughout their lifetime!

Amphibious Life

Hippos spend up to 16 hours a day in the water to keep cool and protect their sensitive skin from the sun.
